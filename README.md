# Magic Number

A mystical Android app that reads your mind! Think of a number, answer a few yes/no questions, and watch as the app magically reveals your secret number.

## How To Play

1. **Think of a number** within the selected range (1-31, 1-63, or 1-127)
2. **Swipe through cards** - each card shows a set of numbers
3. **Swipe RIGHT (YES)** if your number appears on the card
4. **Swipe LEFT (NO)** if your number doesn't appear
5. **Watch the magic** - a mystical animation plays while "reading your mind"
6. **Tap Reveal** - your number is revealed with a dramatic card flip!

## Features

- Intuitive swipe gestures
- Multiple number ranges to choose from
- Beautiful mystical purple and gold theme
- "Reading Your Mind" animation with rotating stars
- Dramatic 3D card flip reveal
- Haptic feedback for immersive experience

## Settings

- **Number Range**: Choose 1-31 (5 cards), 1-63 (6 cards), or 1-127 (7 cards)
- **Number Layout**: Display numbers in ascending order or scattered randomly

## The Secret

This app uses a classic mathematical magic trick based on binary numbers. Each card represents a power of 2, and your answers allow the app to calculate your number by adding up the right values. It's math disguised as magic!

## Requirements

- Android 10 or higher
